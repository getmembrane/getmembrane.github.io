Comments without haters

A solution for companies where haters are a problem, crowdscreen is a crowdsourced service that automatically keep haters out of discussions. Unlike traditional moderation, crowdscreen is mostly automated and gets smarter for each processed comment.

Less moderation

Haters already identified elsewhere won’t have to be moderated again at your site.

Prevention

We identify the haters before they have even left a comment. No one will have to read their foul words.

Brand

Makes sure your brand isn't associated with haters. Sorts out the haters around the clock.

Tech

  1. A user visits your site.
  2. A request is sent to our API.
  3. We answer if this is a hater or not.
  4. Your site can automatically take action.

Using crowdscreen is easy. If your running on any of the bigger platforms, just install the corresponding plugin and you are ready to go. If your platform is custom we have a perfectly documented and fault tolerant API complete with examples.
